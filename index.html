Public Relations and Communications Employment Projections

By Meagan DiPolo

These charts show the long-term growth of communications jobs in Florida and the states with the highest availability of public relations jobs within the next decade. Those looking to go into the field should understand the outlook of these jobs in the next decade. Public relations degrees are often used for various communications jobs, such as reporting or advertising, so it is important for a prospective public relations student to understand their options for post-graduate life. This data set can be also applied to public relations practitioners in Florida who want to evaluate the possibility of getting a job close to home. More data about the outlook of communications jobs and other fields can be found on ProjectionsCentral.com.
